Terms of Tuition

Let’s keep things smooth and enjoyable for both of us!

Watch Video

Lesson Details:

Lessons are held weekly at a regular time unless we’ve agreed otherwise.

Each lesson runs for 30, 45 or 60 minutes.

Fees & Payment:

Lessons prices are:

$120 per 3x 30-minute lesson package,

$180 per 3x 45-minute lesson package or

$240 per 3x 60-minute lesson package

Payment is due weekly before each lesson via bank transfer.

You’ll get a reminder or invoice before payment is due if needed.

Please note: Payments made 7 days after the lesson will incur 15% late payment fees.

Missed Lessons & Makeups:

Life happens! If you need to cancel, please give at least 24 hours' notice.

With enough notice, I’ll do my best to reschedule your lesson during the same week.

Missed lessons without notice may still be charged, unless there’s an emergency.

Public Holdays & School Terms:

I usually follow the NZ school term dates, but I’ll always let you know in advance about any breaks.

Lessons on public holidays are optional.

Practice Expectations:

Regular practice is key to progress. I’ll help guide your child (and you!) on how to make the most of practice time.

Even a little bit each day can go a long way!

Communication:

I’m always happy to chat about progress, goals, or any concerns — just send me a message or email.

Feedback is welcome anytime!

Photo/Video Permission:

Photos and videos are not taken during lessons. If you would like to take a photo/video of your child or the lesson, please let me know beforehand.

Let’s make music an enjoyable and inspiring journey!

By enrolling in lessons, you’re agreeing to these terms — and I’m really looking forward to working with you (and your child)!
